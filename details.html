My Favorite Plants

Here are some of the most fascinating plants growing in my greenhouse:

  • Agave Victoriae-Reginae (Queen Victoria Agave)
    A striking succulent known for its symmetrical rosette of thick leaves marked with white lines.
  • Golden Barrel Cactus (Echinocactus grusonii)
    A spherical cactus famous for its golden-yellow spines and distinct round shape.
  • Agave Americana
    This large succulent has fleshy, pointed leaves and can grow to impressive sizes.
  • Agave Marginata (Variegated Agave)
    A variegated species of agave with beautiful white-edged leaves.
  • Jade Plant (Crassula ovata)
    A popular succulent known for its thick, fleshy leaves and low maintenance care needs.
